About Us

Vintage Rally Club

Founded in 2012, Vintage Rally Club is home to over 150 vintage cars and over 300 members in our crew. Vintage Rally Club was first founded to be a hub where Ghanaians with similar interests can meet and talk about their love for cars. It has prospered into a community and regular showing for all.
